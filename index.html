Our Journey of Faith

From humble beginnings to a thriving community of believers

2015

The Vision Begins

Pastor John and a small group of passionate believers felt God's call to establish a church specifically for the rising generation. What started as a weekly Bible study in a living room became the foundation of our ministry.

2017

First Official Service

We held our first official Sunday service in a rented community center with 25 people in attendance. The vision was clear: to create a space where young people could encounter God authentically and grow in their faith.

2019

Ministry Expansion

Our congregation grew to over 150 members, and we launched our first youth ministry, women's empowerment program, and children's ministry. We also began our community outreach initiatives.

2021

Digital Transformation

During the global pandemic, we quickly adapted to online services and virtual community. This period strengthened our commitment to reaching people wherever they are, leading to our hybrid ministry model.

2023

New Sanctuary

We moved into our current location, a beautiful 500-seat sanctuary that serves as our home base for worship, fellowship, and community events. This marked a new chapter in our growth and impact.

2025

International Impact

Today, Rise Generation International Church serves over 500 members and has planted sister churches in three countries. We continue to grow, adapt, and impact lives for God's kingdom.

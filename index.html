Our Journey of Faith

From humble beginnings to a thriving community of believers

The Vision Begin

Rise Generation International Church was planted in 2017 G.C through the vision of Pastor Abenet Legesse and his Wife Prohetess Kidist Markos,who heard God's call to raise a generation that will seek God and carry His mission.

First Official Service

We held our first official Sunday service in 2017 a rented community center with 15 people in attendance. The vision was clear: to create a space where young people could encounter God and grow in their faith.

Ministry Expansion

Our congregation grew more in members, and God continued to grow the church into great spirtual family,reaching people with gospel and Empoweringthe next generation

Digital Transformation

During the global pandemic, we quickly adapted to online services and virtual community. This period strengthened our commitment to reaching people wherever they are, leading to our hybrid ministry model.

New Sanctuary

By the grace of God, and through the dedication of His people, the goverment has granted us land, and now we are free from rent. This make a significant step forward and give us permanet home to worship train and reach more lives with the Gospel

Rise Generation International Church Impact

Today, Rise Generation International Church serves over 500 members and has planted sister churches around Ethiopia subcities. We continue to grow, adapt, and impact lives for God's kingdom.
